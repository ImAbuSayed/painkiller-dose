[Verse 1]

Kalli raat vich labhda haan hidden clues
Tere jhoothe vaadeyan ne kita mainu damage

[Chorus]
Tu ban meri painkiller dose meri heartbeat

Tere ishq ch hoya haan main thoda jaha hazy

Tenu pyar karda haan main till my death

Zindagi di khed vich taking a ride
